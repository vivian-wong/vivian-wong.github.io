About

Hi! I am Vivian Wong, a Ph.D. student in the Stanford University Civil Engineering department, with a focus in engineering informatics (data analysis and management). I do research in the Engineering Informatics Group. I am currently working on:

  1. data-driven decision-making for infrastructure management
  2. AI applications to smart manufacturing

I received my M.S. in Civil Engineering also from Stanford University in 2019 and my B.S. from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My hometown is Toronto, Canada, but I also spent a great amount of my childhood in the lovely cities of Hong Kong and Beijing.
